About LNG

LNG - The learning zone, is a knowledge sharing and learning platform being developed to share my technical as well as non-technical knowledge I aquired through different experiences in my career and life.

Hi, my name is Anuran Datta. I am from Kolkata and I have almost 2 decade of corporate experiences on application software services with diverse software horizontals and verticals as well as Clients.

I liked the Serverless architectural pattern and concepts, so I started building this site/platform fully on the AWS Serverless echosystem. It is using Angular for SPA, Okta for 3rd party IP and Quarkus for cloud-native Java microservice, deployed as AWS Lambda. Other parts of the architecture depicted in diagram below.
